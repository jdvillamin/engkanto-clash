# Gameplay Reference

## Controls

- `W`, `A`, `S`, `D`: move and jump
- `J`: move 1
- `K`: move 2
- `E`: move 3
- `L`: special move
- `Space`: glide while playing Aswang
- `P`: switch character

Each move has its own cooldown. Stronger moves generally have longer cooldowns
than basic moves.

## Character Moves

| Character | `J` | `K` | `E` | `L` |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tikbalang | Punch | Kick | Dash | Super dash |
| Kapre | Punch | Ground smash | Log throw | Smoke release |
| Aswang | Slash | Slash 2 | Heal | Poison lick |
| Engkanto | Bolt | Flower orb | Vines | Hollow green |

## Character Notes

Aswang can glide by holding `Space` during the falling part of a jump.
